Token Exposure Alert

Polli Leaks is an automated system that scans your repositories for exposed Pollinations tokens and notifies you instantly. Keep your secrets safe and your code secure!

Get Started Learn More

How It Works

  1. Scans your GitHub repositories for sensitive Pollinations tokens.
  2. Detects leaks in real-time and highlights the affected files and lines.
  3. Sends you a beautiful, actionable email alert with all the details.
  4. Helps you remediate and secure your codebase quickly.
