Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Givwy, we collect the following information:

  • Discord user ID, username, and avatar
  • Discord server (guild) information where Givwy is installed
  • Giveaway participation data and preferences
  • Bot usage statistics and command interactions
  • Message content only when directly interacting with bot commands

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain Givwy's giveaway services
  • Process giveaway entries and select winners fairly
  • Send notifications about giveaway status and results
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Prevent fraud and ensure fair giveaway participation
  • Comply with Discord's Terms of Service and API requirements

3. Data Storage and Security

We take data protection seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure with regular backups
  • Access to user data is limited to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly audit our security practices and systems
  • Data is stored for as long as necessary to provide our services

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent
  • With trusted service providers who assist in operating our services

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with relevant authorities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@givwy.com

6. Children's Privacy

Givwy is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
